About JobHack

Built for placement cells and learners who need clarity, not just jobs but also skills. We connect listings, applications, and outcomes so everyone stays aligned from drive announcement to offer letter. Much important for the students to grow and get the best out of the job market.

For students

Browse curated roles, apply with structured forms, and see your — interviews, decisions, and offers — in one timeline. Upload your resume for academic recognition and active tracking. Suggest the sKills neede to acquire for the job market and the students Interested in.

For institutions

Run drives, publish slots, and track the skills of the students and the companies.And understand the requirments for the Job Market so students of their college dont lag behind.

Built for trust

Role-based access, notifications at each step, and audit-friendly records so coordinators can focus on people, not paperwork.
